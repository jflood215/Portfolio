About Me

A quick look into my background, experience, and personality.

With 11 years experience as a Registered Dietitian, I've worked for a large corporation, a highly successful start up, and also created a private practice in Zionsville, IN. Although I really enjoyed my time in the nutrition world and had many fun and successful career moments, I was ready for something new. I had always been curious and intrigued by computer programming and the code behind it, and finally went for it with the help of Eleven Fifty Academy. As challenging, diverse, and ever evolving as web and software development is, I believe I've found my dream career. I love to learn and push myself and am constantly trying to improve my knowldege of coding best practices, and computer science in general.

The programming languages, frameworks, and technologies that I have knowledge or experience with are:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, React, TypeScript, APIs, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Full Stack apps, and Git
