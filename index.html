About Me

A quick look into my background, experience, and personality.

With 10+ years experience as a Registered Dietitian, I've worked for a large corporation, a highly successful start up, and also created a private practice in Zionsville, IN. Although I really enjoyed my time in the nutrition world and had many fun and successful career moments, I knew there was more for me to explore and experience. After deciding to change careers, I enrolled and graduated from the Full Stack Web Development program at Eleven Fifty Academy in October, 2020. After graduation I was able to convince my current employer to let me help the software engineering team, and essentially created an internship for myself at Noom Inc. It was great to spend a few months gaining experience and learning from more experienced developers. I love to learn and push myself and am constantly trying to improve my knowldege of coding best practices, and computer science in general.

The programming languages, frameworks, and technologies that I have knowledge or experience with are:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, Bootstrap, React, TypeScript, APIs, Node.js, Express, Sequelize, PostgreSQL, Redux, and Git
