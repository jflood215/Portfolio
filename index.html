About Me

A quick look into my background, experience, personality, and life before Eleven Fifty.

With 11 years experience as a Registered Dietitian, I've worked for a large corporation, a highly successful start up, and also created and operate my own private practice in Zionsville, IN. Over that time I've worked with a wide range of clients, from those battling cancer and other chronic diseases, to those wanting to lose weight. Along the way, there have been many challenges and successes, and I continue to push forward and grow personally and professionally at every opportunity. My friends and family would describe me as driven, detail oriented, and a perfectionist. Although I had a very gratifying and successful career before enrolling at Eleven Fifty Academy, I knew there was more for me to achieve.

I had always been curious and intrigued by computer programming and the code behind it. Changing careers after so many years seemed daunting, and risky, so I started learning a little on my own by attending the free coding classes from Eleven Fifty, and researching everything I could. Around that time, a good friend of mine attended and graduated from Eleven Fifty and said it's one of the best decisions he's ever made. I love pushing myself to improve, continuing to learn, and becoming the best I can be. As challenging, diverse, and ever evolving as web and software development is, I believe I've found my dream career.
